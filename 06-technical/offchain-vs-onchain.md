# Off-Chain vs On-Chain Responsibility Matrix

This document defines which system responsibilities belong on-chain
and which must remain off-chain.

The goal is to:
- Maintain legal and regulatory compliance
- Preserve human authority and discretion
- Enable cryptographic auditability
- Support reuse by multiple organizations
- Allow seamless transition from LLC to Corporation

This document is designed as a **portable governance template**.
