# Reward Mapping & Incentive Design

This document defines how tasks translate into rewards,
how multiple reward token types may coexist,
and how rewards remain discretionary, auditable, and legally compliant.

Reward Mapping is policy-driven and customizable per organization,
while preserving non-negotiable safety invariants.

---

## 1. Core Principle

```text
Rewards recognize contribution.
Rewards do not promise profit.
Rewards do not confer authority.
