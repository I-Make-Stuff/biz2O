# Governance Playbook (Day-to-Day Operations)

This playbook describes how governance operates in practice.
It translates governance roles, authority, tokens, and incentives
into repeatable weekly and monthly actions.

The goal is to:
- keep work moving forward
- distribute governance labor fairly
- prevent burnout and bottlenecks
- compensate invisible coordination work
- allow easy join/leave from governance roles

This playbook is operational guidance, not a legal contract.
