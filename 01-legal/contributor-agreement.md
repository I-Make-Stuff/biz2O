# Contributor Agreement (Non-Employment, Non-Ownership by Default)

This Contributor Agreement (“Agreement”) defines the terms under which
an individual (“Contributor”) may voluntarily contribute to the Company.

This Agreement is designed to:
- allow low-friction collaboration
- support token-based recognition
- avoid employment or contractor misclassification
- integrate with blockchain or ledger-based systems
- remain compatible with U.S. labor and securities law

This Agreement does not grant ownership, employment, or guaranteed compensation.

---

## 1. Nature of the Relationship

```text
Contribution is voluntary.
Compensation is discretionary.
Ownership is not implied.
