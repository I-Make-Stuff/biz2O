# Governance Roles & Authority Structure

This document defines the governance roles used by the Company,
their scope, responsibilities, authority boundaries, and lifecycle.

The purpose of this document is to:
- enable clear decision-making
- distribute governance labor safely
- allow easy entry and exit from governance
- prevent authority capture or ambiguity
- support compensation via non-wage tokens

This document is legally subordinate to the Operating Agreement or Charter.
