# Member Onboarding & Exit Framework

This document defines how individuals join and leave the Company as Members
(owners), and how ownership interacts with governance, tokens, compensation,
and exit.

The intent is to:
- make it easy to form a company around an idea
- allow contributors to become owners without complexity
- prevent accidental employment or investment misclassification
- allow members to leave cleanly and safely
- preserve trust and continuity

This document is legally subordinate to the Operating Agreement or Charter.
