# Distribution Policy (Discretionary, Allocation-Based)

This Distribution Policy defines how the Company determines,
approves, and executes distributions of value.

Distributions are discretionary and subject to governance approval.
Nothing in this policy guarantees payment, profit, or timing.

This policy is legally subordinate to the Operating Agreement or Charter.
