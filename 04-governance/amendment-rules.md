# Amendment Rules & Evolution Framework

This document defines how the Company may change over time,
which rules are permanent, which are adaptable,
and how amendments are proposed, reviewed, approved, and enforced.

The goal is to:
- protect core principles
- enable responsible evolution
- maintain trust with contributors and owners
- avoid governance deadlock
- support LLC → Corporation conversion
